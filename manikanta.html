Pawan Kalyan

DEPUTY CM

Konidala Pawan Kalyan[3] (born Konidala Kalyan Babu; 2 September 1971[2]) is an Indian politician, actor, filmmaker, action choreographer, and martial artist who has been serving as the 10th deputy chief minister of Andhra Pradesh since June 2024. He is also serving as the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development & Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh. He is the founder and president of the Jana Sena Party.
